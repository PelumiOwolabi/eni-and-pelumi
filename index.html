How it began

Our Story

✦

Some love stories begin with a glance across a crowded room. Ours began over a disagreement about the best shortcut through the city — and neither of us has entirely conceded the point.

We found in each other something rare: a companion for the quiet moments and the loud ones alike. Someone to share morning coffee and long evenings, to argue gently over small things and agree on all the ones that matter.

After years of building a life — of shared kitchens and morning routines, of adventures and ordinary Tuesdays — we are ready to make it official before the people we love most.

We are so glad you are here.
